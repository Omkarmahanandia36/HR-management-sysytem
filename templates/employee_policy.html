Official Company Policy

Please read and understand the rules regarding attendance, leaves, and daily workflows below.

⏱️ Working Hours

  • Standard Shift: {{ settings['workday_start_time'] }} to {{ settings['logout_time'] }}
  • Maximum Expected Work Hours: {{ settings['maximum_work_hours'] }} hours/day
  • Late Mark Threshold: Arriving after {{ settings['late_mark_threshold'] }} will incur a late mark.
  • Check-In Location: Must be within {{ settings['geofence_radius_meters'] }} meters of the office location.

🌴 Leave Allowances

  • Casual Leaves: {{ settings['casual_leave_days'] }} days per year
  • Sick Leaves: {{ settings['sick_leave_days'] }} days per year
  • Leaves must be requested through the portal and approved by HR.
  • Unapproved absences are marked as Absent automatically at the end of the day.

⚠️ Disciplinary Flags

🔴 Red Flag: Given when an employee has (3 or more total leave days OR 3 or more absent days) AND 3 or more total late marks in a month. This represents a combination of frequent absences and frequent tardiness.
🟠 Orange Flag: Given when an employee has 3 or more total leave days in a month, but does not have enough late marks to trigger the Red Flag.
🟡 Yellow Flag: Given when an employee has a streak of 3 or more continuous half-days.
